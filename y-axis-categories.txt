SUMMARY: MOST USEFUL Y-AXIS CLUE CATEGORIES
================================================================================

HIGH YIELD (Many entries fit):
1. "Starts with [A-Z]" - universally applicable
2. "Ends in [A/Y/N/S/ER]" - many options
3. "Contains double letters" - many options
4. "One-word answer" - good for musicians, single-name celebrities, most cities
5. "Contains the letter [Z/X/Q]" - rare letters make interesting clues

MEDIUM YIELD:
6. "Starts and ends with same letter" - works well for places ending in A
7. "Contains a color" - limited but interesting
8. "Contains a common noun" - many last names work
9. "Three-word answer" - works for formal names and places

LOW YIELD (But interesting when applicable):
10. "Alliterative answer" - very few fit
11. "Palindrome name" - almost none
12. "Rhymes with [word]" - requires creative pairing
13. "Contains a number" - very few
